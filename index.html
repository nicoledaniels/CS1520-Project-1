About Me

I've had an interest in computers and programming for as long as I can remember. I started by making websites for my hobbies and favorite television shows as a child. These sites were ugly, but were fun to make. I went to technology camps at Brown and MIT where I delved deep into topics that interested me. In high school, I was one of only two girls who took AP Computer Science. After succeeding in my AP Computer Science class, I knew computer science was what I wanted to study in college.

I am currently a senior studying at the University of Pittsburgh. I am involved with a couple student groups. This year, I was elected as the president of the Pitt Computer Science Club. I am also actively involved in the Women In Computer Science Club.
